A Page with Comments

In addition to the blog articles, you can also integrate the comment function on static pages. To do this, the controller of a page must derive from the 'CommentsController' and override its two properties $route and $templateFile.

In the template of the page you have to include Comments.partial.html.

  • Location of this Controller: 'controllers/TestController'
  • Location of this Template: 'templates/Test.partial.html'
{include 'Comments.partial.html'}
